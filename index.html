DATA ABOUT INSTANT RAMEN

Ramen can be enjoyed in many ways: In a bowl or a cup, with chopsticks or a fork, slurping or sipping, at a restaurant or at home. At his website, theramenrater.com, a self proclaimed ramen judge has been rating ramen for almost 20 years now. Throughout his time, he's collected data in the form of reviews and polls - many of which are quite fascinating. Below are some data-sets about ramen transformed into an interactive and visually friendly visualization about the most beloved way to eat ramen -- at home and instant.

In what form is Instant-Ramen enjoyed the most?

Instant-ramen comes in many different shapes and sizes. This poll shows that people enjoy eating ramen out of packs the best by far. Interestingly, when insant ramen is depicted in media, it often shows ramen in cup form. While ramen may be most recognizable in a cup, it's definitely not the tastiest.
