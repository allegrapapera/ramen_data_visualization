Data about instant ramen

Ramen can be enjoyed in many ways: In a bowl or a cup, with chopsticks or a fork, slurping or sipping, at a restaurant or at home. Below are data-sets transformed into an interactive and friendly visualization about the most beloved way to eat ramen -- at home and instant.

In what form is Instant-Ramen enjoyed the most?

Instant-ramen comes in packs, bowls, cups, and trays. This poll shows that people enjoy eating ramen out of packs the best. Then bowls, then cups, and then trays.
